Boosting your productivity with the new espresso Display 15 and Stand+

Boosting your productivity with the new espresso Display 15 and Stand+
Written by  
Dom O'Brien
February 24, 2025

Have you ever been on the road, trying to juggle multiple tasks on your laptop’s small screen, wishing you had the same setup as your office? I know the feeling all too well. That’s why I want to share something that’s been a game-changer for me: the espresso Display 15 and its handy companion, the Stand+.

‍

A Big Screen That Fits in Your Bag

Imagine carrying a 15.6-inch Full HD screen with you wherever you go. Sounds bulky, right? Not with the Espresso Display 15. It’s designed with premium materials that make it both durable and lightweight—perfect for slipping into your backpack or briefcase without weighing you down. So whether you’re heading to a coffee shop or catching a flight for a business trip, you’ve got a portable monitor that’s ready to go.

Plug and Play Simplicity

Now, you might be thinking, “Adding an extra monitor sounds complicated.” But here’s the kicker: it connects to your laptop with just a single USB-C cable. That’s it! No need to fuss with multiple cords or hunt for extra power outlets. Just plug it in, and you’re all set to expand your workspace in seconds.

Flexibility with Stand+

Setting up in a new place every day can be a challenge, but Stand+ makes it effortless. It’s an ergonomic stand that lets you position your monitor exactly how you like it. Need it at eye level? Done. Prefer a tilted angle? You got it. It’s sturdy yet easy to pack up, so you can maintain good posture and comfort no matter where you’re working.

Work Anywhere Like You’re at the Office

What I love most about this setup is how it lets me maintain my productivity on the go. I can have my emails open on one screen while working on a report on the other, just like I do at my desk. It’s amazing how much more efficient you can be when you have that extra screen real estate, especially when you’re away from your usual workspace.

Perfect for People on the Move

If you’re a business traveler, a digital nomad, or just someone who likes to switch up their work environment, the espresso Display 15 and Stand+ are definitely worth considering. They’ve made a huge difference for me, turning any location into a productive workspace.

Give It a Try

So, if you’re tired of feeling cramped on a single screen and want to boost your productivity wherever you are, why not give the espresso Display 15 and Stand+ a shot? It might just change the way you work on the go.
